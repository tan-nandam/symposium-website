Heads

Dr. Raghava V Cherabuddi

Dr. Raghava is a BE (CSE), MS (Computer Science), MS (Computer Engineering) and PhD (Computer Engineering, USA). He was most recently with Microsoft Corporation, Hyderabad as an Academic Alliance Manager for nearly two years. Previously he was with Intel Corporation in the US for about 12 years. He has published over 10 papers in international conferences. After living in the USA for 16 years he relocated back to India to contribute to the technological advancement of Andhra Pradesh.

Dr. Nayanathara K Sattiraju

Dr. Nayanathara K Sattiraju obtained her BE (ECE) and ME from the College of Engineering, Osmania University in 1983 and 1985 respectively. She is a recipient of the CMC gold medal in ME. She obtained her doctorate from the National Institute of Technology, Warangal in 2003. She was the Principal of SKTRM College of Engineering for ten years prior to joining CVR College of Engineering as Professor and Head of ECE.

Ms. Vijaya Mair

Vijaya Mair has been with CVR for last 6 years and her arrival has given a new direction for the placement activities of the college. The number of placements showed continuous upward trend. Many new placement initiatives were undertaken by her which are really praiseworthy. She developed personal relationship many of the top companies.

Prof. L.C. Siva Reddy

Completed his M.Sc. in Physics, M.Phil. in Computational Techniques from University of Hyderabad. Earlier he was HOD of Department of CSE at Osmania University, Hyderabad. He was responsible for developing software for entrance tests, result processing of LAWCET, MBACET, MCACET, EAMCET - 85 and 89. Served as Chairman, Board of Studies, Osmania University, Hyderabad. He joined CVRCE during 2001 and has been serving as HOD, CSE Department and Vice Principal of the College.
